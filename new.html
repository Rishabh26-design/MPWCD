Laadli Laxmi Yojana

Madhya Pradesh State Scheme

Description

A flagship scheme of MP government to improve birth rate of girls, their health and education.

Key Benefits

  • ₹30,000 fixed deposit in girl's name at birth
  • Annual scholarships for education
  • ₹1 lakh at age 21 if completing 12th grade
  • Free medical treatment up to ₹25,000

Eligibility

Girl children born in MP after 1st April 2006 to families with annual income less than ₹2 lakh.
